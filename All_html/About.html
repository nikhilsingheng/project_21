About Us

At Fresh meal, we build products & solutions that redefine the food ordering & delivery space in India, every single day. The best part? Every bit of your work at Fresh meal will help us change the way India eats!

Whats the story of Fresh meal ?

With love and support from consumers, Fresh meal expanded far and wide, first through the entire city of Bengaluru and then across the entire country. Today, Fresh meal is the leading food ordering and delivery platform in India.
